ABOUT

This royal club has been founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club,
The word Real is Spanish for "ROYAL" and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 together with the royal crown in the emblem.
The team has played its home matches in the 85,454-capacity Santiago Bernabeu Stadium in downtown Madrid since 1947.

ACHIEVEMENT

The best club of the 20th Century FIFA Trophy

Real Madrid closed out the year 2000 by being named the Best Club of the 20th Century by FIFA.Di Stéfano and Florentino Pérez were in charge of collecting the trophy, which was presented to them during a ceremony in Rome. Real Madrid were awarded with the accolade having won the voting by more than 42%.

European Cups

Real Madrid hold the record for the most victories, having won the competition 11 times, including the inaugural competition. They have also won the competition the most times in a row, winning it five times from 1956 to 1960. Real Madrid was the first champion team in 1995. YEARS- 1955-56 1956-57 1957-58 1958-59 1959-60 1965-66 1997-98 1999-00 2001-02 2013-14 2015-16

EUROPEAN SUPER CUP

Real Madrid wins the European super cups for three times in 2002 2014 2016.

UEFA CUPS

Real Madrid has won the UEFA Cup twice times.
The 1984–85 UEFA Cup was won by Real Madrid on aggregate over Videoton.
The 1985–86 UEFA Cup was won by Real Madrid on aggregate over 1. FC Koln. This was the first season in which English clubs were serving an indefinite ban from European football competitions, which meant two-time UEFA Cup winners Liverpool and Tottenham Hotspur, as well as Southampton and Norwich City were unable to compete.

LATIN CUPS

Real Madrid participated in the 1955 and 1957 editions, winning both times. Di Stefano led that fantastic squad, a team no other European side could beat.

FIFA CLUB WORLD CUP

Real Madrid won their first FIFA Club World Cup after defeating San Lorenzo 2–0 in the final in 2014.
